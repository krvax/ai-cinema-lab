# FinOps for AI Cinema

## Purpose

AI-generated media can incur significant costs through video rendering, image generation, storage, and experimentation.

AI Cinema Lab applies FinOps principles to creative workflows.

## Cost Controls

### Budget Validation

- Budget thresholds defined in configuration.
- Rendering blocked when limits are exceeded.

### Asset Reuse

- Reuse generated assets whenever possible.
- Minimize regeneration costs.

### Experimentation Strategy

1. Prototype using free tools.
2. Validate concepts.
3. Render final assets using paid models.

## Future Enhancements

- Cost dashboards
- Budget alerts
- Cost attribution per campaign
- Automated reporting
